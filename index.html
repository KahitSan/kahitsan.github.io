About KahitSan

KahitSan means "anywhere" in Filipino—because we believe productive work can happen anywhere when you have the right environment. We're Naga City's most affordable co-working space, created to serve students, freelancers, entrepreneurs, and anyone seeking a productive space without the premium price tag.
